Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event?

For large events like weddings and corporate galas, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance. For smaller setups, 1 month notice is usually sufficient.

Do you provide custom decoration designs?

Yes! We specialize in bespoke designs. Our creative team will work closely with you to understand your vision and craft a customized mood board and setup.

Are there special requirements to book online?

To ensure security and streamline our process, you must create a verified account using your Ethiopian phone number before accessing the booking portal.
