Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Jafe Decor. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and secure your information when you visit our website, create an account, or book an event with us.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Details: Full name, father's name, grandfather's name, and nationality (collected during registration).
  • Contact Information: Ethiopian phone numbers (+2519...) and email addresses.
  • Event Information: Dates, venue details, guest counts, and specific requirements provided in the booking form.
  • Payment/Debit Information: Account numbers provided solely for the purpose of securing event reservations.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To register your account and provide secure access to our booking portal.
  • To process event bookings, communicate setup details, and execute your event flawlessly.
  • To respond to inquiries submitted via our contact form.
  • To manage our Affiliate Program payouts and communications.

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data. All user accounts are protected by secure, numeric passwords. Your debit account information is processed securely and is strictly used for booking reservations. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data stored in our systems. If you wish to close your Jafe Decor account, please contact our support team.
